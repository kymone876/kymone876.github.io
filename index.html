Biography

Raised in a single parent household, Kymone found her passion for hair early on in life by practicing on her younger siblings and members of the community. As a teen she perfected her craft and after leaving high school she enrolled into a beauty school at her local community college. She started recieving recognition by doing the hair of local celeberities and business people.
